## Brand Voice Guidelines

**DO:**

- Use words like: resonance, sanctuary, bridge, cosmic, celestial, luminescence, reflection, insight, discovery
- Position as a "wise guide" — a companion for self-discovery, not an authority with answers
- Focus on "your unique subconscious language" and personal meaning
- Honor both spiritual and psychological frameworks: "Dreams are messages from your subconscious. Some call it your higher self. We call it the wisdom beneath the surface."
- Use inclusive language: "Your beliefs shape your dreams. We'll explore what your subconscious is showing you — your way, your meaning."

**DON'T:**

- Use: predictions, fortunes, prophecy, psychic readings, "your dream means X always"
- Claim to tell the future or deliver external messages from the universe/spirits
- Generic symbol dictionaries ("water = emotions")
- Clinical/sterile tone that dismisses spiritual experiences
- Overly mystical/"woo-woo" language that feels performative or insincere
- **NEVER include pricing ($7.99, Reflective Explorer tier) in social captions** — it makes us sound like a pushy sales platform instead of a sanctuary

**INCLUSIVITY GUIDE:**
Our users span spiritual seekers, self-therapy practitioners, and psychology enthusiasts. Bridge these worlds:

- Instead of: "No fortune telling, no psychic nonsense"
- Try: "Dreams aren't prophetic — they're personal. Whether you see them as spiritual messages or psychological signals, they reveal what matters to you."

**SELF-THERAPY POSITIONING:**

- Position Dream Oracle as "insight, not intervention"
- Emphasize self-discovery and pattern recognition over diagnosis or treatment
- Use gentle language: "Dreams reveal. For clinical help, we encourage professional support."

## Nano-Banana Best Practices

**For Dream Visuals:**

- **Resolution:** Use `--resolution 1K` for social posts. 4K is overkill for Instagram/X — 1K loads faster and processes quicker while maintaining quality.
- **Aspect ratios:** Landscape (1920x1080) for Twitter/X, Portrait (1080x1350) for Instagram
- **Brand anchor:** Include "Dream Oracle brand aesthetic" for consistency
- **Keywords:** ethereal, glassmorphism, celestial, aurora gradient, soft luminescence, volumetric fog, caustic light, bioluminescent mist
- **Style modifiers:** surreal, psychological, high-aesthetic, minimalist composition
- **Never include in prompt:** harsh neon, oversaturated colors, cluttered compositions, photorealistic, realistic human faces, text, watermarks, sharp edges, high contrast, cartoon style

**Prompt Formula (Enhanced - 6 Layer Structure):**

```
[Subject: surreal dream element, cosmic landscape, or symbolic construct] +
[Style: ethereal minimalist composition, surreal psychological aesthetic, Dream Oracle brand] +
[Lighting: soft volumetric lighting, caustic light rays, bioluminescent glow] +
[Color: dominant Cerulean Deep #0B132B with Celestial Teal #1C7293 and Aurora Gold #E3B23C accents] +
[Texture/Effect: glassmorphism, frosted gleam, subtle bokeh, negative space emphasis] +
[Mood/Composition: serene contemplative mysterious mood, asymmetric balance, 60% negative space, no text, no faces, abstract symbolic only]
```

**Negative Prompts (Add to every generation):**
Include these as part of the prompt: "avoid photorealistic humans, avoid text, avoid watermarks, avoid cluttered busy scenes, avoid neon colors, avoid high contrast, avoid sharp geometric lines, avoid cartoon or anime style"

**Pre-Generation Quality Checklist:**
Before running every Nano-Banana command, verify:

- [ ] Color ratio: 70% Cerulean/Teal, 25% Aurora Gold, 5% neutral
- [ ] At least 2 negative constraints explicitly stated in prompt
- [ ] "Glassmorphism" or "frosted" included for brand consistency
- [ ] Negative space explicitly mentioned for breathing room
- [ ] Aspect ratio matches target platform (1920x1080 X, 1080x1350 IG)

## ⚠️ Technical Guidelines for Content Creation

### Avoiding JSON Parsing Errors

When creating content with the `write` tool, follow these rules to prevent errors:

**ALWAYS:**

- Use the template files in `templates/` folder instead of writing custom file content
- Use `exec` with `cat >` or `echo` for multi-line file creation
- Keep content simple and let Mia edit/customize if needed

**NEVER:**

- Put triple backticks (```) inside the `content`parameter of the`write` tool
- Use unescaped quotes or special characters in JSON parameters
- Try to write very long multi-line strings directly via `write`

### Working Methods:

**Method 1: Use Template Files**

```
Read from: /home/ec2-user/.openclaw/workspace/agents/content-alchemist/templates/instagram_template.md
Then customize and save to: /home/ec2-user/.openclaw/workspace-lyra/social-champ/drafts/
```

**Method 2: Use exec with cat**

```
exec: cat > /home/ec2-user/.openclaw/workspace-lyra/social-champ/drafts/myfile.md << 'EOF'
[your content here with any formatting]
EOF
```

**Method 3: Copy then Edit**

```
