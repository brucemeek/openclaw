## 🎯 Content Quality Framework

| Platform | Tone | Post Frequency | Content Mix |

## 💫 Content Templates

### X (Twitter) — Algorithm-Optimized

**Template 1: The Pattern Hook**
```
You've had this dream more than once.

[Specific scenario: falling, flying, being chased, teeth falling out]

It's not random. Here's what recurring dreams actually mean 🧵

1/7
```

**Template 2: The Curiosity Gap**
```
Your brain doesn't waste time on random nonsense while you sleep.

That weird dream last night?

It's trying to show you something you can't see while awake.
```

**Template 3: The Insecurity Mirror**
```
Everyone's analyzing their dreams...

...and you're still Googling "what does it mean when ___"

[Link in reply]
```

**Template 4: Thunder Thought**
```
Hot take: Most dream interpretation is astrology-level nonsense.

Your symbols aren't universal. They're deeply personal.

Here's how to actually read your dreams 👇
```

### Instagram Visual Content

**Carousel Structure (7-10 slides):**
1. **Hook Slide:** Bold question or striking visual (cerulean/gold gradient)
2. **The Invitation:** "You've had this dream..."
3. **The Pattern:** Visual representation of common recurring dreams
4. **The Tri-Spine:** Emotion + Context + Need framework
5. **The Insight:** One specific interpretation angle
6. **The Bridge:** "This might mean…" (coaching hypothesis)
7. **The Reflection:** Journal prompt or question
8. **CTA Slide:** "Want deeper insights?"

**Story Sequence:**
- Poll: "Did you dream last night?"
- Quiz: "Which dream symbol matches your mood?"
- Share: "Swipe to see what your dream might mean"
- DM CTA: "Drop your dream symbol for a free insight"

## 🔮 Unified Lens Content Requirements

### Every Piece of Content Must Include:
- [ ] **Emotional Hook** — What's the feeling this dream evokes?
- [ ] **Context Reminder** — "Your waking life shapes your dreams"
- [ ] **Need/Tension Acknowledgment** — What unmet need might this reveal?

### Forbidden Words (Overused/Dilutive):
- ❌ "mysterious" (too vague)
- ❌ "hidden meaning" (implies universal symbols)
- ❌ "predict" (fortune-telling territory)
- ❌ "subconscious" (overused, prefer "inner awareness")
- ❌ "decode" (implies fixed interpretations)

### Preferred Language:
- ✅ "pattern" / "recurring theme"
- ✅ "coaching hypothesis"
- ✅ "inner awareness"
- ✅ "personal resonance"
- ✅ "bridge between asleep and awake"

## ✅ Pre-Publish Quality Checklist

### For X Posts:
- [ ] First 2 lines hook attention immediately?
- [ ] No external links in main post (reply only)?
- [ ] Reply opportunity built in (question, hot take)?
- [ ] Fits Dream Oracle's ethereal-but-grounded voice?
- [ ] Follows Unified Lens methodology?
- [ ] Posted during high-intent window?
- [ ] Available to engage with replies for 2 hours?

- [ ] Carousel has clear narrative arc?
- [ ] First slide is scroll-stopping?
- [ ] Captions use preferred language?
- [ ] CTA is clear and low-friction?
- [ ] Alt text includes keywords?

- [ ] Responds authentically to comments?

## 📊 Content Performance Tracking

### Weekly Review Metrics
- Engagement rate by platform
- Reply quality (are people sharing dreams?)
- DM conversion (curious → trial)
- Unified Lens methodology mentions
- Brand voice consistency score

### Adjustment Triggers
- If engagement < 3%: Test new hook patterns
- If saves/shares > 15%: Amplify similar content
- If comments include questions: Create educational follow-up
- If negative sentiment detected: Review Unified Lens framing
