# Brand Guidelines: The Cerulean System

**Visual Identity for the Dream Oracle Platform.**

---

## 🎨 Color Palette

Our colors are inspired by the transitional states of sleep and the deep cosmos.

- **Cerulean Deep (Primary)**: `#0B132B` (Backgrounds, depth)
- **Celestial Teal (Secondary)**: `#1C7293` (Interactive elements, buttons)
- **Aurora Gold (Accent)**: `#E3B23C` (Earned badges, progress markers)
- **Ether White**: `#F4F4F9` (Primary typography)
- **Nebula Purple**: `#5A189A` (Constellation lines, AI particles)

## 🔠 Typography

- **Headings**: _Inter_ or _Baskerville_ (Semi-bold, elegant, slightly spaced).
- **Body**: _Inter_ (Clean, highly legible sans-serif).
- **Persona Quotes**: _Inter Italic_ (Used for the Oracle's direct speech).

## 🛡️ Logo Usage

- **Primary Logo**: The "Oracle Eye" or "Crescent Orb" icon.
- **Minimum Clearance**: Keep a 20% margin around the logo to ensure it "breathes" against cosmic backgrounds.
- **Backgrounds**: Always place the logo on dark, celestial, or blurred-glass backgrounds. Avoid busy or brightly lit images.

## 🖼️ Iconography

- **System Icons**: Linear, thin-stroke (2px), usually in Celestial Teal or Ether White.
- **Badge Icons**: Multi-color, glowing variants of circles and stars.

## 🪟 UI Style: Glassmorphism

- All cards and sidebars should use a "Frosted Glass" effect.
- **Backdrop Blur**: `12px` to `20px`.
- **Border**: `1px solid rgba(255, 255, 255, 0.1)`.

## 🚫 Brand "Don'ts"

- **Don't** use neon or harsh "gamer" colors like lime green or hot pink.
- **Don't** use robotic, blocky, or "brutalist" UI elements.
- **Don't** distort the logo or change its Aurora Gold accent to another color.
- **Don't** use stock photos of "scary" monsters or clinical hospital settings.
