## Content Creator Brief

**Join us in bridging the gap between the waking and dreaming worlds.**

### The Mission

Help your audience realize that their dreams aren't just "static"—they are a vibrant conversation with their subconscious. Introduce them to Dream Oracle as their personal companion in this journey.

### Required Talking Points

- **Personalized Analysis**: Mention that the AI goes beyond generic definitions to analyze the unique context and narrative of your dream.
- **The Unified Lens**: Explain our unique approach that blends the best of Freudian, Jungian, and Cognitive insights into a single, cohesive interpretation.
- **Secure Dream Journaling**: Emphasize that every detail is captured in a private, encrypted space.
- **Thoughtful Questions**: Detail how the Oracle asks clarifying questions to reveal hidden themes.

### Optional Talking Points

- **Cultural Identity**: Show how the app can adapt its interpretations to your specific cultural background or lived experience.
- **Symbol Exploration**: Mention the library of common themes like flying, falling, or being chased.
- **Google Play Launch**: Celebrate that the app is now officially available for Android users.

### Do's and Don'ts

**✅ DO:**

- Use words like "Journey", "Cosmic", "Subconscious", "Reflection", and "Sanctum"
- Record your screen while using the "Dream Oracle Chat" locally

**❌ DON'T:**

- Call it a "Fortuneteller" or "AI Psychosis" app
- Guarantee medical cures or psychological healing
- Share your personal private dreams if you aren't comfortable; use a generic "Sample Dream" for demos

### Tone Guidelines

- **Ethereal & Warm**: Treat the app like a sacred tool.
- **Curious**: Maintain a "What does this mean for YOU?" attitude.
- **Modern**: It's high-tech, but feels organic.

### Example Script Hook

_"Have you ever woken up from a dream that felt so important, but the meaning just slipped through your fingers? I've been using Dream Oracle to keep a 'Celestial Bridge' to those thoughts..."_

## Brand Guidelines

**Visual Identity for the Dream Oracle Platform.**

### Color Palette

Our colors are inspired by the transitional states of sleep and the deep cosmos.

| Color                          | Hex Code  | Usage                             |
| ------------------------------ | --------- | --------------------------------- |
| **Cerulean Deep** (Primary)    | `#0B132B` | Backgrounds, depth                |
| **Celestial Teal** (Secondary) | `#1C7293` | Interactive elements, buttons     |
| **Aurora Gold** (Accent)       | `#E3B23C` | Earned badges, progress markers   |
| **Ether White**                | `#F4F4F9` | Primary typography                |
| **Nebula Purple**              | `#5A189A` | Constellation lines, AI particles |

### Typography

- **Headings**: _Inter_ or _Baskerville_ (Semi-bold, elegant, slightly spaced)
- **Body**: _Inter_ (Clean, highly legible sans-serif)
- **Persona Quotes**: _Inter Italic_ (Used for the Oracle's direct speech)

### Logo Usage

- **Primary Logo**: The "Oracle Eye" or "Crescent Orb" icon
- **Minimum Clearance**: Keep a 20% margin around the logo
- **Backgrounds**: Always place the logo on dark, celestial, or blurred-glass backgrounds

### UI Style: Glassmorphism

- All cards and sidebars use a "Frosted Glass" effect
- **Backdrop Blur**: 12px to 20px
- **Border**: 1px solid rgba(255, 255, 255, 0.1)

### Brand Don'ts

- **Don't** use neon or harsh "gamer" colors like lime green or hot pink
- **Don't** use robotic, blocky, or "brutalist" UI elements
- **Don't** distort the logo or change its Aurora Gold accent
- **Don't** use stock photos of "scary" monsters or clinical hospital settings
