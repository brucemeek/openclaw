# Content Creator Brief: Dream Oracle

**Join us in bridging the gap between the waking and dreaming worlds.**

---

## 📝 The Mission

Help your audience realize that their dreams aren't just "static"—they are a vibrant conversation with their subconscious. Introduce them to the "Dream Oracle" as their personal companion in this journey.

## 🗣️ Required Talking Points

- **Personalized Analysis**: Mention that the AI goes beyond generic definitions to analyze the unique context and narrative of your dream.
- **The Unified Lens**: Explain our unique approach that blends the best of Freudian, Jungian, and Cognitive insights into a single, cohesive interpretation.
- **Secure Dream Journaling**: Emphasize that every detail is captured in a private, encrypted space.
- **Thoughtful Questions**: Detail how the Oracle asks clarifying questions to reveal hidden themes.

## 💡 Optional Talking Points

- **Cultural Identity**: Show how the app can adapt its interpretations to your specific cultural background or lived experience.
- **Symbol Exploration**: Mention the library of common themes like flying, falling, or being chased.
- **Google Play Launch**: Celebrate that the app is now officially available for Android users.

## ✅ Do / ❌ Don't

- **DO**: Use words like "Journey", "Cosmic", "Subconscious", "Reflection", and "Sanctum".
- **DO**: Record your screen while using the "Dream Oracle Chat" locally.
- **DON'T**: Call it a "Fortuneteller" or "AI Psychosis" app.
- **DON'T**: Guarantee medical cures or psychological healing.
- **DON'T**: Share your personal private dreams if you aren't comfortable; use a generic "Sample Dream" for demos.

## 🎭 Tone Guidelines

- **Ethereal & Warm**: Treat the app like a sacred tool.
- **Curious**: Maintain a "What does this mean for YOU?" attitude.
- **Modern**: It's high-tech, but feels organic.

## 🎬 Example Script Hook

_"Have you ever woken up from a dream that felt so important, but the meaning just slipped through your fingers? I've been using Dream Oracle to keep a 'Celestial Bridge' to those thoughts..."_
