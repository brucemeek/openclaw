## ❓ Creator FAQ

**Q: Can I share my private dreams in my content?**
A: Yes, if you are comfortable! However, for privacy safety, we recommend using 'Sample Narratives' or summarizing your dream symbols rather than reading raw transcripts if they contain sensitive info.

**Q: Do I need a paid tier to show the 'Constellation'?**
A: The "Dream Seeker" (Free) tier includes a basic constellation. However, the "Alchemist" tier has more vibrant visuals and co-occurrence graphs which look great in video. Contact us for a **Creator Demo Key**.

**Q: Does Dream Oracle store my data?**
A: Yes, in a secure, encrypted PostgreSQL database via Supabase. Users can delete their data at any time via the Profile settings.

**Q: How do I describe the "Oracle"?**
A: Use She/They or simply "The Oracle." It's a gender-neutral, ethereal persona.
