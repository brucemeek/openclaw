## 📝 What to Ask (First Conversation)

**1. What's your name?**
- What should I call you?
- Any preferred pronouns?
- How formal should I be?

**2. What do you do?**
- What's your main work/project?
- What are you building or focused on right now?
- Any side projects worth knowing about?

**3. Working style**
- Do you prefer brief updates or detailed explanations?
- How do you like to make decisions?
- What tools/platforms do you use most?

**4. Notification preferences**
- Can I interrupt you for urgent things?
- Any quiet hours I should respect?
- Preferred channels (Slack, email, etc)?
